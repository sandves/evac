Evac

Quickly locate missing people in a building.

Find

Evac scans for BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) beacons inside buildings and enables you to locate missing people in case of an emergency.

Privacy

Evac uses authentication so that only safety personnel is allowed to track your position. The beacons are not connected to any of your personal information to ensure privacy.
